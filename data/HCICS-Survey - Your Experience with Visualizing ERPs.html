Your Experience with Visualizing ERPs

We are interested in your experiences with ERP visualisation. We would like to know more about how research and clinical practitioners, both novices and experts, use EEG plotting tools in their daily work. We will publish the results of this survey in order to describe and improve current visualisation techniques.

This survey is anonymous. If you wish to enter a prize draw or receive further information about the results of the study, you will be asked separately for your email address. Your responses will not be linked to this email address. There are no known risks associated with taking part in this study. Participants may withdraw from the study at any time.

You will have the chance to win one of three Muse S2 Gen EEG headsets.

This questionnaire will take approximately 15-30 minutes to complete. You can go back to previous pages. You can interrupt your session at any time by clicking on the 'Resume later' button in the top right hand corner. Remember that the session expires in 4 hours, so you will lose your answers if you do not complete or save the survey.

Some questions require you to write your opinion. We recommend that you take the survey on your PC.

If you have any further questions, please contact us:

Benedikt.Ehinger@vis.uni-stuttgart.de
